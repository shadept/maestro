- FIX (flaky tests): DONE — both CHORE-entry WATCH flakes were REAL product bugs, not test artifacts. (1) TurnQueue per-session FIFO VIOLATED (reproduced 8/12 runs, three different tests, e.g. same-session t2 starting before t1): FUR-13's `boss.work()` encoding spawns localConcurrency=cap independent pollers; SKIP LOCKED lets poller B claim a session's second turn while poller A holds its first, then client-side localGroupConcurrency admission runs in fetch-response ARRIVAL order — whichever response lands first runs, the earlier turn gets restore()d and starts late.
